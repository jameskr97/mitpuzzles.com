### Light-up Rules

- Light Up is played on a rectangular grid. The objective is to place light bulbs on the grid so that every white square is lit yellow.
- A light bulb lights up all squares in its row and column, except for those blocked by black squares.
- The numbers in black squares indicate how many light bulbs must be horizontally or vertically adjacent to that square (not diagonally).
- The beams of light bulbs can cross each other, but two light bulbs cannot be in direct line of sight of each other (i.e., they cannot see each other directly without a black square in between).

### How to Play

- Click on a cell to toggle between the state of empty and light bulb <i class="md-cell md-cell-lightup-bulb bg-yellow-200 border rounded"></i>.
- Place the light bulbs in such a way that all white squares are lit and the conditions of the black squares are satisfied.
- You will be able to submit your solution once all the white squares are lit.
