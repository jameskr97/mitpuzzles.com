### Minesweeper Rules

- Each number in the grid represents the count of adjacent cells that contain a mine, including diagonals.
- A mine is marked with a flag <i class="md-cell md-cell-flag"></i>.
- A safe cell is marked with a cross <i class="md-cell md-cell-empty"></i>.

### How to Play

- Your goal is to correctly mark each of the cells <i class="md-cell md-cell-unrevealed"></i> as safe
  <i class="md-cell w-full md-cell-empty inline-block mt-[2px]"></i> or flagged <i class="md-cell md-cell-flag"></i>.
- Click the cell cycle through the states, (right click to cycle in reverse).
- You will be able to submit your solution once there are no more unmarked <i class="md-cell md-cell-unrevealed"></i> cells left.
