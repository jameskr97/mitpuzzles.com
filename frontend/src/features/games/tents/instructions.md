### Tents Rules

- Tents is a logic puzzle where you must place tents adjacent to trees in a grid.
- The numbers outside the grid indicate how many tents are in that row or column.
- Tents cannot touch each other, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
- A tent can only be placed adjacent to a tree. A tent can be placed next to multiple trees, but that tent can only be paired with one tree.

### How to play

- Click on an empty cell to mark it as a tent <i class="md-cell md-cell-tent"></i>, grass <i class="md-cell bg-green-300"></i>, or empty.
- Each cell must be marked as either a tent or grass. (Grass cells are marked as green).
- You can click the number in the outer row or column to mark all cells in that row or column as grass.
- You will be able to submit your solution once all the cells are marked as either a tent or a green.
